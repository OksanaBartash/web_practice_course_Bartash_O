My responsive website about My weaknesses

  I think only nature is perfect in everything. Every person, no matter how accomplished or capable, has weaknesses. I am not a perfectionist, so I have weaknesses:
Procrastination: Sometimes it is difficult for me to manage my time effectively or maintain focus on tasks. But I read a lot of information about how I need to learn and control my focus.
Also I spoke with a psychologist about it and she gave me advice - now every evening I made a detailed plan for the next day. It helps me and really I can see what I did and if I can’t do everything I analyze and draw conclusions.
Lack of self-confidence: Sometimes I lose lack of belief in my abilities especially if something goes wrong. I have a family who always supports me.
Also I try following advice from successful people - working on yourself, fighting with my fears which prevent me from taking risks, pursuing my goals.
Poor stress management: It depends on situations, sometimes I get a lot of stress. It is impossible to live life and not experience stress! Of course, I try to find a way out and solve the problem, or drink a sedative and try to find a way out and solve the problem again.
What doesn't kill us makes us stronger)))
I am - Oksana Bartash know and recognize and understand these weaknesses to strive for personal growth and improvement.
